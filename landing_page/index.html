Who are we?

We are a group of software students from Holberton, who are driven to learn and help.

What did we make?

In this project we developed a semi-decentralized web application for the users of the metropolitan public transport, they can at the moment of getting on a bus and without internet access: get to know and travel around Montevideo in an easy and accessible way.

How do we make it?

We design a system to serve dinamic web-apps on the router allowing us to have a descentralized system.
